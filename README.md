👔 Your Role: AI Transformation Consultant at Tata iQ
You step into the shoes of a consultant within Tata iQ's Financial Services team. Your mission:
Analyze customer data, predict delinquency risks using AI-driven techniques, and design an ethical, scalable collections strategy for Geldium Finance.
You are not just building models — you are bridging the gap between raw data and boardroom decisions. 
📚 The 4 Core Tasks (Modules)
Task 1: Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) with GenAI
Objective: Assess data quality, identify risk indicators, and structure insights for predictive modeling.
What You Do: Use GenAI tools to conduct EDA on customer financial data with delinquency flags.
Skills Built: Data Quality Management, Exploratory Data Analysis, AI Analytics, Analytical Reporting.
Mindset Shift: GenAI doesn't replace analysis — it amplifies insight generation at scale. 
Task 2: Predicting Delinquency with AI
Objective: Design a no-code predictive modeling framework to assess customer delinquency risk.
What You Do: Propose structured risk-scoring logic, transparent decision pathways, and evaluation criteria aligned with business goals — all before writing a single line of code.
Skills Built: Predictive Analytics, Model Selection, Model Validation.
Key Lesson: The best models are the ones non-technical stakeholders understand and trust. 
Task 3: Business Report & Data Storytelling
Objective: Turn AI insights into a stakeholder-ready business report to guide delinquency reduction efforts.
What You Do: Craft a compelling narrative for the Head of Collections at Geldium, translating metrics into actionable strategy.
Skills Built: Business Communication, Data Interpretation, Strategic Thinking, Decision Making. 
Task 4: Implementing an AI-Driven Collections Strategy
Objective: Design a comprehensive, ethical, and scalable collections strategy using agentic AI.
What You Do: Architect AI automation that reduces bias, meets regulatory compliance, and balances business needs with customer empathy.
Skills Built: AI Strategy, Ethical Reasoning, Regulatory Compliance, Process Automation.
Key Lesson: Responsible AI isn't an afterthought — it's designed from Day One.
